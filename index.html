About Digital Wealth Management

What is Digital Wealth?

Almost everyone has heard of Bitcoin. If you currently own a bunch of Bitcoins, then you have digital wealth.

Buying some Bitcoins sounds very simple to those of us with the knowledge. However, there are many individuals and entities who just don't know how and don't have the time or the resources to learn. What they do know, is that they would like to buy some.

Digital Wealth Management removes the need to know by providing a simple, safe and private way to get involved in the crypto world by providing procurement, liquidation, custody and distribution services for any and all digital assets including Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, and a myriad of NFTs.

  • The first and most famous REAL virtual item was Bitcoin.
  • Ethereum was the first programmable blockchain.
  • NFTs are the newest digital asset.
  • Web3 and the Metaverse. The new decentralised web.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

  • How do I become a DWM client?
    Send us an email and we'll call you direct. One of our representatives will then guide you through our KYC and AML procedures. This takes about 48 hours depending on where in the world you live. Then you will have direct access to a member of our team who you can work with and tell what you want to do.
  • I already have some crytocurrency that I want to sell can you help?
    Yes. Send us an email and we'll quickly get you onboard and then one of our team will be at your disposal.
  • I want to buy NFT's in a sale that is coming up, can you help.?
    Of course. Contact us and we will sign you up. Then we'll work closely with you to ensure you don't miss the sale.
  • Is your guarantee backed by a legal contract?
    Indeed it is. Once you're onboard, our London based Law Firm will draw up a legally binding contract complete with guarantee.
  • I have a requirement for a regular purchase of cryptocurrency or stable coin. Can you assist?
    Yes. We are happy to purchase any amount of cryptocurrency or stablecoin for you on a regular basis.
